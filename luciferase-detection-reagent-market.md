<p><p>The luciferase detection reagent market is divided into two main types: single luciferase detection reagents and double-luciferase detection reagents. Single luciferase detection reagents are designed for measuring the activity of one specific luciferase enzyme, providing straightforward quantification in applications like gene expression studies. In contrast, double-luciferase detection reagents enable the simultaneous measurement of two different luciferase enzymes, facilitating more complex analyses and comparative studies of promoter activity or cellular responses, thus enhancing experimental efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
