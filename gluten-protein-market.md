<p><p>The gluten protein market consists of three main types: wheat gluten, corn gluten, and other gluten sources. Wheat gluten, derived from the protein found in wheat, is popular for its elasticity and is widely used in baking and food products. Corn gluten, obtained from corn processing, serves as a protein supplement in animal feed and is valued in the food industry for its binding properties. Other gluten sources encompass various grains and legumes, contributing to niche applications in food and non-food industries.</p></p>
