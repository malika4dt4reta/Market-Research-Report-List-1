<p><p>The wheelchair anti-tippers market consists of two main types: forward anti-tippers and rear anti-tippers. Forward anti-tippers are designed to prevent wheelchairs from tipping forward, enhancing user safety during incline transitions or sudden movements. Conversely, rear anti-tippers aim to stabilize the wheelchair and prevent backward tipping, particularly during sudden starts or stops. Both types contribute to overall wheelchair stability, allowing users to navigate various terrains confidently while reducing the risk of accidents and providing greater independence.</p></p>
