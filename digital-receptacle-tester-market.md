<p><ul><li>GFCI Receptacle Tester</li><li>AFCI Receptacle Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Receptacle Tester market encompasses two main types: GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter) receptacle testers and AFCI (Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter) receptacle testers. GFCI testers are designed to evaluate and verify the functionality of GFCI outlets, which protect against ground faults and electrical shocks. AFCI testers, on the other hand, assess the performance of AFCI outlets that prevent arc faults, reducing the risk of electrical fires. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and compliance in electrical installations.</p></p>
