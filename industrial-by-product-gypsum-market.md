<p><p>The industrial by-product gypsum market consists of several types: Phosphogypsum, a by-product from phosphate fertilizer production, is used in construction and agriculture. Fluorogypsum, generated from the production of hydrofluoric acid, serves similar applications. Flue Gas Desulfurization Gypsum results from cleaning emissions in power plants and is used in drywall and cement. Gypsum Citrate is derived from phosphoric acid production and has applications in soil amendments. Salt Gypsum arises from salt production processes, often used in agricultural settings.</p></p>
