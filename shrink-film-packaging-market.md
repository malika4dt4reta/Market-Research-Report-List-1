<p><p>Shrink film packaging is categorized into two main types: printed and unprinted shrink film. Printed shrink film features graphics, branding, or designs, enhancing product visibility and marketing appeal. It is commonly used for retail packaging, promotional items, and product differentiation. Unprinted shrink film, on the other hand, serves a more functional purpose, providing protection and containment without additional decoration. This type is often utilized for bulk packaging, ensuring products remain intact during storage and transportation while maintaining a clean, simple appearance.</p></p>
