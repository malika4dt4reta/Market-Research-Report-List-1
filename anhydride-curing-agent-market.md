THPA is favored for its low viscosity and good adhesion properties, making it ideal for various industrial applications. PA is widely used in epoxy resins and coatings for its excellent chemical resistance. Other types may include cycloaliphatic anhydrides and maleic anhydrides, catering to niche applications and specific performance requirements in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
