<p><p>Laboratory cell sorting equipment encompasses various technologies that isolate specific cells for analysis. Fluorescent Activated Cell Sorting (FACS) utilizes fluorescent markers to identify and separate cells based on their optical properties. Magnetic-Activated Cell Sorting (MACS) employs magnetic beads linked to antibodies for targeted cell capture. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) and microfluidics integrate microscale fluid manipulation to facilitate precise sorting, allowing for high-throughput applications. Each method contributes uniquely to cell biology research and clinical diagnostics by enhancing sorting efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
