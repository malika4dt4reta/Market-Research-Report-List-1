<p><strong>The NIR-SWIR Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NIR-SWIR imaging cameras encompass two primary types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture full frames at once, ideal for applications requiring high spatial resolution and rapid image acquisition, such as quality control in manufacturing. Conversely, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them suitable for continuous processes, such asweb inspection and traffic monitoring. Both types leverage near-infrared and short-wave infrared wavelengths for enhanced material detection and analysis, expanding their application range across industries.</p></p>
