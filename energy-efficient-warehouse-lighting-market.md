<p><p>The energy-efficient warehouse lighting market comprises several types, including High-Intensity Discharge Lamps (HID), Linear Fluorescent Lamps (LFL), and Light Emitting Diodes (LED). HID lamps provide high brightness for large spaces but consume more energy. LFLs are more energy-efficient than traditional lighting but have shorter lifespans. LEDs are leading the market due to their exceptional energy efficiency, longevity, and decreasing costs. Other lighting types may include compact fluorescent and smart lighting systems, catering to diverse operational needs in warehouses.</p></p>
