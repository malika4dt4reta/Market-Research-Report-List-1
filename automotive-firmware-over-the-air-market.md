<p><ul><li>Cellular</li><li>Wi-Fi</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Firmware Over The Air (FOTA) market is primarily divided into two types: Cellular and Wi-Fi. Cellular FOTA utilizes mobile networks to deliver firmware updates to vehicles, ensuring seamless updates even in remote areas. Conversely, Wi-Fi FOTA allows updates to be downloaded when vehicles are parked within range of a wireless network, often offering faster transfer rates and lower costs associated with data transmission. Both types enhance vehicle performance, security, and compliance with evolving automotive technologies.</p></p>
